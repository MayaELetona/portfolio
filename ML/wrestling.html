This is Maya.

Hi there, welcome to my wrestling bio page:) I joined the boys wrestling team as a freshman in highschool, it wasnt until my sophmore year that we recruited a few more women and formed a womens wrestling team. In my senior year of highschool I applied to college hoping to achive my dream of attending an Ivy League school.

Fast forward a year and I am in NYC attending Columbia University, here I wrestled four years under Columbia Women's Wrestling Club Team. I made national teams and traveled the world representing Team USA.

As a recent grad I am enrolled in USA Wrestling's Silver Coaches Certification Program. I plan to continue my professional wrestling career and further my coaching education as an assitant coach at Columbia Women's Wrestling Club.
